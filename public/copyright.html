Content Copyright

Last Updated: 26th November 2021

  1. Parties

    This Content Copyright will apply to you as a Content Buyer (also known as Brand in the marketplace) or Content Creator when a job is agreed between the two parties and content is delivered (known as Delivered Content) in Content Fly marketplace unless the Content Buyer and Content Creator separately agree in writing.

  2. Starting date of the Agreement

    This Agreement will become effective between the Content Buyer and Content Creator on the date when the Content Creator transfers the content deliverable to the Content Buyer.

  3. Intellectual Property Rights

    If the Content Buyer has agreed to purchase the Delivered Content created by the Content Creator, then upon receipt of the payment agreed by the Content Buyer and Content Creator, the Content Creator hereby assigns to the Content Buyer all Intellectual Property Rights in the Delivered Content. In the case intellectual property rights owned by a third party is involved, the Content Creator must specify it to the Content Buyer during the transfer of the deliverable and let Content Buyer know about the available options to obtain the third party content license.

  4. Liability of Content Fly

    Content Fly is not a party of this Agreement and shall not be liable or responsible for any breaches of this Agreement by any party of the Agreement.
